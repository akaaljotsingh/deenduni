BHAI GADIYA JI FOUNDATION (BweI gVIAw jI sMsQw) is a registered non-governmental, non- profit, organization with an objective to support people from the weaker socio-economic background of society and to provide humanitarian aid in disaster areas and civil conflict zones around the world. The organisation is based upon the Sikh principle of “SPREADING LOVE” .

About Bhai Gadiya Ji

Bhai Gadiya Ji was disciple of sixth Sikh Guru, Dhan Dhan Sri Guru Hargobind Sahib. Bhai Gadiya Ji use to visit Jammu & Kashmir frequently to collect Daswand & to inquire about the wellness of J&K people. Bhai Gadiya Ji was known to remain in a state of perpetual ardour and divine ecstasy while performing the tasks ordained by the guru.
