About Us

BHAI GADIYA JI FOUNDATION (BGJF) is a registered non-governmental, non-profit, organization with an objective to support people from the weaker socio-economic background of society and to provide humanitarian aid in disaster areas and civil conflict zones around the world.

The foundation draws its name from the sixth Sikh Guru, Dhan Dhan Sri Guru Hargobind Sahib Jiâ€™s disciple, Bhai Gadiya Ji, who was known to remain in a state of perpetual ardour and divine ecstasy while performing the tasks ordained by the guru. The philosophy of his devout life is at the centre of all projects undertaken by the organisation. Challenging as it is, for unversed men to work selflessly, it is important that there be a permanent fountainhead of energy and knowledge whence they can continue to receive guidance and support. DHAN DHAN SRI GURU GRANTH SAHIB JI is the fountainhead of Bhai Gadiya Ji Foundation and all the activities are carried out in the light of 'GURBANI'.

Although the nucleus of its activities and campaigns lies in the communities living in the strife-torn region of Jammu and Kashmir, the foundation seeks all-around empowerment of all strata of human society. To this end, BGJF has been working incessantly in the fields of skill-oriented education, communal harmony, sustainable employment, environment, and healthcare and youth affairs. The foundation has received an immense response and developed a credible base amongst the communities of Jammu and Kashmir. With the blessings of WAHEGURU, JI BGJF has built-up a dedicated team of around 150 volunteers and patrons, including students, doctors, teachers and business owners and it has been going from strength to strength since its inception.
